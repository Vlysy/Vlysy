Resume Best Practices

  • Tailor each resume to the specific job
  • Use action verbs to begin bullet points
  • Quantify achievements with numbers when possible
  • Keep formatting consistent throughout
  • Limit to 1-2 pages for most positions
  • Include keywords from the job description
  • Prioritize relevant experience over chronological order
  • Proofread carefully for typos and errors
