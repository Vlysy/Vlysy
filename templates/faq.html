General Questions

Resume Analyzer is an AI-powered tool that helps you improve your resume by providing detailed feedback, suggestions, and corrections. The tool analyzes your resume for common issues, evaluates its effectiveness, and gives you practical recommendations to make it more attractive to employers.

Our tool uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your resume content. Here's how it works:

  1. Upload your resume (PDF, DOCX, or TXT) or paste your resume text
  2. Select your preferred language (English or German)
  3. Our AI analyzes your resume for style, content, grammar, and format issues
  4. You receive a comprehensive analysis with specific suggestions
  5. Apply the improvements and download your enhanced resume

Yes, we take data privacy seriously. Your resume information is used only for analysis purposes and is never shared with third parties without your consent. Your data is processed securely, and we do not store your personal information longer than necessary for providing our service.

Features & Functionality

Our tool supports the following file formats:

  • PDF (.pdf)
  • Microsoft Word (.docx)
  • Plain Text (.txt)

You can also paste your resume text directly into our text area if you prefer not to upload a file.

Currently, our tool supports:

  • English
  • German

We are working on adding support for additional languages in the future.

The Anschreiben (cover letter) generator is a feature that automatically creates a personalized German-style cover letter based on your resume and a job description. To use this feature:

  1. First analyze your resume
  2. Go to the Anschreiben page
  3. Paste the job description you're applying for
  4. Generate your customized cover letter
  5. Download the generated Anschreiben as a text file

Technical Support

If you're having trouble uploading your file, please try these steps:

  1. Check that your file is in a supported format (PDF, DOCX, or TXT)
  2. Ensure your file size is under 10MB
  3. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  4. If using a PDF, make sure it's not password-protected
  5. As an alternative, copy-paste your resume text into the text area

If you continue to experience issues, please contact us for support.

Your resume score is calculated based on several key categories:

  • Content (25%): Quality and relevance of information
  • Format (25%): Layout, organization, and visual appeal
  • Language (25%): Grammar, spelling, and word choice
  • Conciseness (25%): Clarity and efficient communication

Score ranges:

  • 90-100: Excellent - Your resume is highly effective
  • 70-89: Good - Minor improvements needed
  • 50-69: Average - Several improvements recommended
  • Below 50: Needs significant improvement
